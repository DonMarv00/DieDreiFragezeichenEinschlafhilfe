This App helps you to select a random episode of the audiobook "Three Investigators"

Functions:

- Different options to select a episode
- Over 350 Episodes in 4 categories, updated frequently
- Custom settings to select a episode

Privacy:

The app offers a function that opens Spotify to play a audiobook.
Spotify may collect personally identifiable information.
Read the Spotify privacy policy to know how Spotify uses your data.

Privacy Policy of Spotify can be found here:

https://www.spotify.com/de/legal/privacy-policy/

The function can be disabled in the app settings.
